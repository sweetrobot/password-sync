Password Sync for Mac - Installation Instructions
================================================

Thank you for downloading Password Sync!

INSTALLATION:
1. Double-click "Password-Sync.dmg" to open the installer
2. Drag the "Password Sync" app to your Applications folder
3. Open Applications folder and double-click "Password Sync" to launch

FIRST TIME SETUP:
Since this app isn't signed with an Apple Developer certificate, macOS may block it initially.

If you see "Password Sync can't be opened because it is from an unidentified developer":
1. Right-click (or Control+click) on the Password Sync app in Applications
2. Select "Open" from the context menu
3. Click "Open" in the dialog that appears
4. The app will now run normally

WHAT THIS APP DOES:
- Securely merge Apple and Google password exports
- All processing happens locally on your Mac (no data sent anywhere)
- Export merged passwords in multiple formats
- Identify and resolve password conflicts between services

USAGE:
1. Export your passwords from Apple Keychain (CSV format)
2. Export your passwords from Google Password Manager (CSV format)
3. Upload both files to the app
4. Review the merge results and conflicts
5. Export your unified password collection

SECURITY:
- No internet connection required
- All data stays on your computer
- No passwords are transmitted or stored by the app

Need help? The app includes built-in guidance and error messages.

Enjoy your unified password management!
